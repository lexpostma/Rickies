MagTrickies

The magnetic trophies for listeners.

For listeners at home there’s the MagTricky, the official way to keep up with who has won the Rickies. A 10cm (4”) flat magnet featuring the majestic Connected globe artwork allows the passionistas to track who currently holds Chairman titles with small cylindrical magnets. Because magnets are awesome.

Unlike the official Tricky, there are two smaller silver 5mm magnets for when the titles are dispersed, and one larger golden 7mm magnet for the consolidated chairman, or “Mega Chairman”.
